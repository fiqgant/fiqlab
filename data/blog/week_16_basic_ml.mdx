## Introduction
The final week of the course is dedicated to reviewing the material covered throughout the course and preparing for the final exam. Students will have the opportunity to reinforce their understanding of the concepts and techniques learned in the preceding weeks and to solidify their knowledge in preparation for the final assessment.

## Objectives
- To review and reinforce the concepts and techniques learned in the preceding weeks
- To prepare for the final exam and assess their understanding of the material covered in the course
- To evaluate their progress and identify areas for further improvement

## Outline Lesson
### Lesson 1: Review of the course material
- Recap of key concepts and techniques learned in previous weeks
- Summary of important definitions, formulas, and algorithms
- Overview of practical applications of the concepts and techniques

### Lesson 2: Preparation for the final exam
- Review of exam format and requirements
- Discussion of sample questions and possible approaches to solving them
- Preparation of a study plan to help students succeed on the final exam

### Lesson 3: Final exam
- Assessment of students' understanding of the material covered in the course
- Evaluation of students' progress and identification of areas for further improvement

## Activities
- Review of the course material through class discussion, individual or group projects, and self-study
- Preparation for the final exam through practice questions and sample exams
- Final exam to assess students' understanding of the material covered in the course.


Note: The final exam will be a comprehensive assessment of students' understanding of the material covered in the course, including both theoretical and practical elements. The exam will be designed to evaluate students' ability to apply the concepts and techniques learned in the course to real-world problems.
